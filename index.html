Oriclean Private limited is a division of ORIGROUP. The company has diversified with other divisions like Oritrade Private limited having interest in manufacturing and trading the products of mass consumption.

Location

Oriclean Private Limited Plant is situated at a distance of about 20 kms from Cuttack. Due to close proximity to plant, transportation cost is very minimal as it is well connected. The Plant is fully mechanized.
